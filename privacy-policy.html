Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 5, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 5, 2026

EstateLink, operated by Ronnel Estrada (PRC Accred. No. 007584 | DHSUD Accred. No. CVR-A-02/19-2104), is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you share with us through our Facebook Page, Messenger, advertisements, and website at ronnelestrada.com.

By interacting with our page or messaging us, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

01

Information We Collect

When you interact with EstateLink through Facebook Messenger, our ads, or our website, we may collect the following:

  • Your name and Facebook profile information (as made public)
  • Contact details you voluntarily share (phone number, email address)
  • Your responses to automated chat questions (e.g., property interest, budget range, preferred contact method)
  • General location information (e.g., city or region)
  • Any other details you share during our conversations
02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your property inquiries and provide relevant listings
  • Send you property details, assume unit information, and investment breakdowns you requested
  • Follow up on your interest in EstateLink's real estate advisory services
  • Improve how we communicate and serve future clients

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

03

How We Store Your Information

Your information may be stored in our Messenger inbox, CRM tools, and secure contact lists. We retain your information only as long as necessary to serve your inquiry or as required by applicable Philippine law, including the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173).

04

Facebook & Messenger

When you interact with us through Facebook or Messenger, Facebook's own privacy policy also applies. EstateLink's automated chat collects your responses only to qualify and serve your inquiry. We do not access any data beyond what you voluntarily share in the conversation.

For Facebook's data practices, visit: facebook.com/privacy/policy

05

Your Rights Under Philippine Law

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have the right to:

  • Be informed about how your data is used
  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Request erasure of your data, subject to legal limitations
  • Withdraw consent to our use of your data at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly using the details below.
